About This Tool

Sid Meier's Civilization V is a landmark 4X strategy game that allows you to guide a civilization from the dawn of man into the space age. With dozens of leaders, a variety of victory conditions, and countless map settings, the number of ways to play is nearly infinite. This can make starting a new game a daunting task.

This game randomizer is built to solve that problem. It eliminates the time spent deliberating over game settings and throws you right into a new and unexpected scenario. By randomizing everything from your leader and map type to the victory condition you should aim for, this tool encourages you to adapt your strategies and explore different facets of the game. It's an excellent way for both new and veteran players to experience everything Civilization V has to offer.

How to Use the Randomizer

Click the "Generate All" button to instantly create a full game setup. If you wish to change a specific setting, use the individual re-roll buttons (↻) for that category. You can also choose whether to include DLC content for leaders and maps using the checkboxes provided.

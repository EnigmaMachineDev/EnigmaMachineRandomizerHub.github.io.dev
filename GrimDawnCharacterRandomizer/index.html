    About This Tool

    Grim Dawn is a rich and complex action RPG, celebrated for its deep dual-class system that allows for a vast number of character build combinations. With nine distinct masteries to choose from, players can mix and match to create a unique hero. However, this wealth of options can often lead players to stick with what they know.

    This character randomizer is designed to push you out of your comfort zone. It generates a random dual-class combination, a weapon focus, and key abilities to build around. This encourages you to explore the intricate synergies between different masteries and skills that you might have overlooked. Whether you're a seasoned veteran looking for a fresh challenge or a new player wondering what to try next, this tool is a perfect way to dive into the dark world of Cairn with a new perspective.

    How to Use the Randomizer

    Click the "Generate Character" button to receive a random class combination, a set of suggested focus abilities, and a primary weapon type. Use the individual re-roll buttons (↻) to change any part of the build you don't like. Let the results inspire your next adventure!

        About This Tool

        Elden Ring, the critically acclaimed action RPG from FromSoftware, offers a vast world and an incredible diversity of weapons, armor, and skills. This immense freedom can sometimes lead to decision paralysis or the tendency to stick with familiar playstyles. This is where the Elden Ring Character Randomizer comes in.

        This tool is designed to inject unpredictability and creativity into your playthroughs. By generating a completely random character build—from your primary weapon and armor set to your talismans and spells it encourages you to adapt and master equipment you might have otherwise ignored. It's the perfect way to discover new synergies, tackle the Lands Between from a fresh perspective, and create unique role-playing scenarios for your Tarnished.

        How to Use the Randomizer

        Simply click the "Generate Character" button to get a complete build. If you're not happy with a specific part of the build, you can use the individual re-roll buttons (↻) for each category to fine-tune your loadout without starting from scratch. Each item is linked to its respective Fextralife wiki page, allowing you to quickly get more details on its stats and location.

About This Tool

Warhammer 40,000: Space Marine 2 thrusts you back into the role of Captain Titus, an Ultramarine, as you battle hordes of Tyranids. The game features a wide array of iconic weapons and different classes, each offering a unique way to purge the xenos. With so many tools of destruction at your disposal, it can be easy to fall back on your favorite boltgun and chainsword.

This loadout randomizer is designed to force you out of that tactical comfort zone. It assigns you a random chapter, class, and a full set of weapons, creating a unique challenge for your next mission. This tool encourages you to master every weapon in the Emperor's arsenal and adapt your strategies on the fly. It's a perfect way to add replayability and discover new, effective ways to serve the Imperium.

How to Use the Randomizer

Click the "Generate Loadout" button to get a completely random setup, including your chapter, class, and weapon loadout for your next mission. Use the individual re-roll buttons (↻) to change any part of the loadout. For the Emperor!

About This Tool

Homebrewing wine is a deeply rewarding hobby that combines science and artistry. From selecting your fruit to controlling the fermentation process, every choice impacts the final product. For those who enjoy making country wines, meads, and other fermented beverages, there is a world of ingredients to explore beyond traditional grapes.

This randomizer is for the adventurous homebrewer looking to step outside their usual recipes. It generates a random type of fruit or base ingredient, along with target levels for sweetness, carbonation, and alcohol content. This tool can help you discover your next favorite recipe, challenge you to work with new ingredients, and add an element of surprise to your craft. Will you make a dry, sparkling raspberry wine or a sweet, still mango wine? Let the dice decide!

How to Use the Randomizer

Click the "Generate Wine" button to get a full recipe concept. If you have a specific ingredient in mind but want to experiment with its style, you can use the individual re-roll buttons (↻) to fine-tune the parameters. Use this tool as the starting point for your next delicious homebrewing project.

About This Tool

Soulframe is the upcoming fantasy MMORPG from Digital Extremes, the creators of Warframe. While much is still shrouded in mystery, it promises a deep connection to nature, deliberate and tactical combat, and a world rich with history. As with any complex RPG, players will be faced with countless choices for their gear and abilities.

This loadout randomizer is based on what has been revealed about the game so far. It's designed to spark theorycrafting and inspire ideas for the kinds of characters you might want to play when the game releases. By generating a random combination of Pacts, armor, and weapons, it gives you a creative starting point. Will you be a knight bound to a whimsical fawn, wielding a greatsword? Or a druidic figure with a staff and a pact to a fierce wolf? Let your imagination run wild as we all await the arrival of Soulframe.

How to Use the Randomizer

Click the "Generate Loadout" button to get a full set of randomized gear and a Pact. Use the individual re-roll buttons (↻) to change any specific part of the loadout. As more information about the game is released, this tool will be updated with new items and possibilities.

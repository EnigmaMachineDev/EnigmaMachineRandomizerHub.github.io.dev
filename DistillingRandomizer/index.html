About This Tool

The world of distilled spirits is vast and fascinating, with centuries of tradition and innovation behind every bottle. From the complex smokiness of a Scotch whisky to the botanical bouquet of a craft gin, there is a spirit for every occasion and taste. This tool is for distillers, connoisseurs, and the curious alike who wish to explore this rich landscape.

This randomizer helps you discover a new spirit to try, study, or even distill. It pulls from a wide range of categories, including whiskeys, rums, vodkas, gins, tequilas, brandies, and more. Let it be your guide to the art and science of distillation.

How to Use the Randomizer

Click the "Generate Spirit" button to get a random spirit type. Use the individual re-roll button (↻) to get a new selection. Whether you're planning a tasting, looking for a new cocktail ingredient, or seeking inspiration for your own distilling projects, this tool is a great place to start.

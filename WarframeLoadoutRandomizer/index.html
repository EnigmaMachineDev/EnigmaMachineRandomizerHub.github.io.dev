About This Tool

Warframe offers an incredible variety of loadout options with dozens of Warframes, hundreds of weapons, and countless companion choices. This randomizer helps you break out of your comfort zone and discover new playstyles you might never have tried otherwise.

Whether you're a veteran Tenno looking for a fresh challenge or a newer player wanting to explore the game's vast arsenal, this tool generates complete randomized loadouts including your Warframe, weapons, companion, Focus school, and even Archwing equipment. Each randomization is an opportunity to experience Warframe in a completely new way.

How to Use the Randomizer

Click the "Generate Loadout" button for a complete, randomized build. You can use the individual re-roll buttons (↻) for any category you'd like to change. Each item links to the Warframe Wiki, so you can easily find details about stats, acquisition methods, and builds.

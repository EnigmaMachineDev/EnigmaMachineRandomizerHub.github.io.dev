About This Tool

Every artist, from the hobbyist to the seasoned professional, faces the creative block: what should I make, and how should I make it? Sometimes, the sheer number of possibilities can be paralyzing. Should you sketch with charcoal, paint with oils, or try a digital medium? What size should your canvas be?

This Artwork Medium Randomizer is designed to be a simple but powerful tool to break through that creative block. By generating a random art medium and a canvas size, it provides a starting constraint to spark your imagination. Constraints are a well-known catalyst for creativity, forcing you to think outside the box and approach your art from a new angle. Whether you're looking to experiment with a medium you've never tried or just need a little push to get started, this tool can help you begin your next masterpiece.

How to Use the Randomizer

Click the "Generate" button to get a random medium and canvas size. If you have a specific canvas in mind but want to try a new medium, you can use the individual re-roll buttons (↻) to adjust the result. Let the random suggestion guide your next creative session!

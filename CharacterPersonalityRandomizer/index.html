About This Tool

Whether you're a writer developing your next protagonist, a tabletop RPG player creating a new adventurer, or just looking for a bit of creative fun, crafting a compelling character is a rewarding challenge. A truly memorable character is defined by their unique traits, quirks, and flaws.

This Character Personality Randomizer is a tool to help you brainstorm and develop rich, multi-dimensional characters. It provides a randomly generated name, gender identity, and a core personality archetype complete with positive and negative traits. Instead of starting from a blank page, you get an instant foundation of ideas to build upon. Use this tool to spark inspiration for backstories, motivations, and internal conflicts that will bring your fictional characters to life.

How to Use the Randomizer

Click the "Generate Character" button to get a new identity and personality profile. You can re-roll each section individually if you want to mix and match. The personality result links to a helpful list of traits, giving you more food for thought as you flesh out your character's story.
